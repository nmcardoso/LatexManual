       All the predefined length parameters (see Predefined lengths) can be used in the arguments of the box-making commands.    \mbox:Horizontal boxes.   \fbox and \framebox:Put a frame around a box.   lrbox:An environment like \sbox.   \makebox:Box, adjustable position.   \parbox:Box with text in paragraph mode.   \raisebox:Raise or lower text.   \savebox:Like \makebox, but save the text for later use.   \sbox:Like \mbox, but save the text for later use.   \usebox:Print saved text.
