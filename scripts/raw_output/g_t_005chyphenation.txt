        Synopsis:   \hyphenation{word-one word-two}   The \hyphenation command declares allowed hyphenation points with a - character in the given words.  The words are separated by spaces.  TeX will only hyphenate if the word matches exactly, no inflections are tried.  Multiple \hyphenation commands accumulate.  Some examples (the default TeX hyphenation patterns misses the hyphenations in these words):   \hyphenation{ap-pen-dix col-umns data-base data-bases}
