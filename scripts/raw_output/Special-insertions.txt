        LaTeX provides commands for inserting characters that have a special meaning do not correspond to simple characters you can type.    Reserved characters:Inserting # $ % & { } _ ~ ^ \   Symbols by font position:Inserting font symbols by number.   Text symbols:Inserting other non-letter symbols in text.   Accents:Inserting accents.   Additional Latin letters:Inserting other non-English characters.   \rule:Inserting lines and rectangles.   \today:Inserting todays date.
