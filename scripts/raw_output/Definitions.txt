       LaTeX has support for making new commands of many different kinds.     \newcommand & \renewcommand:(Re)define a new command.   \providecommand:Define a new command, if name not used.   \newcounter:Define a new counter.   \newlength:Define a new length.   \newsavebox:Define a new box.   \newenvironment & \renewenvironment:Define a new environment.   \newtheorem:Define a new theorem-like environment.   \newfont:Define a new font name.   \protect:Using tricky commands.
